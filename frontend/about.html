Purpose

Why the platform exists

Pension administration is sensitive, document-heavy, and deadline-dependent. UPS PensionsGo was designed to reduce delay, protect records, and make every stage easier to monitor.

Operational efficiency

It replaces fragmented manual follow-up with structured workflow stages, delegated tasks, automated status tracking, and guided review routines.

Transparency and traceability

It keeps application status, file movement, payroll presence, and claims history visible to the right users with an auditable system record.

Better pensioner service

Pensioners can view information relevant to them, follow application progress, understand their account state, and access approved guidance content.

Capabilities

What the platform brings together

The system is not a single-purpose form tool. It is a connected operating environment for pension processing, control, and reporting.

Application workflow

Verification, authorization, write-up, file creation, data capture, assessment, audit, and approval.

Registry and file tracking

Controlled file registry, movement history, shelf availability, and box allocation governance.

Benefits and planning

Benefits estimation, budgeting, arrears tracking, accountability checks, and financial-year forecasting.

Payroll oversight

Payroll uploads, reconciliation against registry data, monthly visibility, and mismatch analysis.

Security and governance

Role governance, audit trails, session controls, backup and restore, export tooling, and activity monitoring.

Guidance and communication

Internal messaging, alerts, public pension guidance, staff knowledge support, and pensioner-facing updates.

Process View

How a pension case progresses

The platform structures work into a monitored journey so responsibility is visible and progress can be tracked at each stage.

01

Submission and verification

Retirement records are initiated, reviewed, and prepared for workflow processing.

02

Controlled staff workflow

Tasks move through write-up, file creation, data capture, assessment, audit, and approval.

03

Registry and payroll linkage

Approved cases populate the registry, payroll visibility, and downstream monitoring tools.

04

Pensioner service and compliance

Pensioners can view status, claims position, life certificate standing, and account conditions.

Users

Who the platform serves

Access is structured by responsibility. Each user sees only the tools and data required for the role.

Operational staff

Clerks, data entrants, write-up officers, file creators, assessors, auditors, and approvers manage workflow actions and supporting records.

Leadership and oversight

OC/Pension, Deputy OC/Pension, and administrators monitor queues, performance, file control, settings, and audit activity.

Pensioners and the public

Pensioners access information relevant to their own records, while the public can view approved informational content and guidance.

Governance

Security, privacy, and control

Pension data is sensitive. The platform is structured around controlled access, monitored actions, and administrative visibility for critical operations.

Role-based access

Users operate within permissions tied to workflow, administration, and pensioner-service responsibilities.

Auditability

Critical actions, imports, exports, deletions, and workflow events are recorded for reference and accountability.

Data continuity

Backup, restore, recycle-bin patterns, and structured cleanup tooling support safer administration over time.
