Calculation Guide

  • Use verified service dates where available.
  • Monthly salary should reflect the pensionable final salary.
  • Early Retirement and Discharge (A.O.R) follow the same rule set: they require either 20 years of service, or at least 10 years of service with age 45 years or above, and they do not attract benefits below 10 years of service.
  • Discharge (C.B.E), Discharge (U.B.E), and Discharge (Public Interest) attract short-service gratuity below 10 years, then full Mandatory Retirement benefits at 10 years or more.
  • Voluntary and Old Age follow the Mandatory Retirement formula family.
  • End of Contract and Discharge (T.X) use the contract gratuity rule only.
  • Discharge (Marriage) attracts marriage gratuity only, without monthly or full pension.
  • Abolition of Office uses its own special 25% formula set.
  • Results are estimates and should be validated before approval.
