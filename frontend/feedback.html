Submit Feedback

Ready to send a formal feedback record?

Open the submission form when you are ready to provide a clear issue, request, suggestion, or service concern.

  • Use references such as file number, workflow stage, or payroll period where relevant.
  • Provide enough detail for operational follow-up without sharing secrets or unrelated sensitive data.
  • Submissions are recorded with a reference number for traceability.
What happens next
1. Intake

Your submission is recorded with a reference number for traceability.

2. Routing

It is directed to the relevant support, operational, or administrative review path.

3. Follow-up

Where a response is needed, the team uses the contact details you provide.

Expected content quality
  • State the exact issue, request, or suggestion.
  • Include identifiers such as file number or process stage where relevant.
  • Indicate whether the matter affects a pensioner, staff workflow, or the public portal.
  • Keep the message factual, concise, and specific enough for follow-up.
